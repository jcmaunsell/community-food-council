We are an all-volunteer food pantry.

Help us make a difference for families in need.

Need food?

Come by Wednesdays and Fridays 10am-12pm for free groceries.

Bring proof of Suffolk County residence, like an ID or a utility bill with your address.

Want to help?

We need volunteers Wednesdays and Fridays from 8:45am-12pm to pick up and deliver food, and to provide groceries to our clients.

Contact us at volunteer@comfoodcouncil.org or 631-351-1060. Or just show up!

Find us at our new location by the Huntington LIRR station!

We are located on the ground level in the back of the north parking garage (60 Broadway). You can drive or walk up from Broadway.

From the south parking garage (94 Lenox Rd), you can cross the tracks via the bridge and take the elevator or stairs down to ground level.

Look for our gray door!
